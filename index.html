About VillaMart

VillaMart is introducing a PPAAS model, a tech-enabled direct-to-consumer marketplace for farm produce. The model uses advanced technology to ensure freshness, reduce wastage, and provide efficient sales tracking. It operates on a franchise basis, benefiting roadside vendors, kirana stores, farmers, FPOs, and SHGs. The model aims to transform traditional agriculture supply chains into a dynamic, technology-driven system.
