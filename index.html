About VillaMart

VillaMart introduces a groundbreaking PPAAS (PhyGital Platform As A Service) model, reshaping the agricultural landscape through a Tech-Enabled Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Omni-channel marketplace for Farm Produce. Our seamlessly integrated mobile and static outlets, coupled with a sophisticated Procurement cum Fulfillment Center (PFC), utilize advanced technology to ensure freshness, chemical-free produce, efficient sales tracking, and pattern analysis, with a primary focus on reducing wastage. Crucially, the PPAAS model operates on a franchise basis, extending its benefits to roadside vegetable vendors, kirana stores, farmers, Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), and Self-Help Groups (SHGs). This inclusive ecosystem aims to create a win-win situation for all stakeholders. The PPAAS model recognizes the significance of a tactile shopping experience for farm products, delivering the same "touch & feel" encounter to consumers, whether at their doorstep or within a convenient walking distance.

Award & Recognition

Throughout our journey, spanning the past few years, VillaMart has been honored with numerous awards and recognitions at various levels, ranging from the village to the state, central, and even international stages. These accolades serve as testament to our dedication and commitment to excellence in all aspects of our operations. We are profoundly grateful for the remarkable opportunities and recognition bestowed upon us, and we remain steadfast in our mission to deliver nothing short of excellence. Your continued support and trust in VillaMart inspire us to strive for greater heights. Thank you for being a part of our journey.

Future Food Fellowship

VillaMart has been chosen as one of the 20 international organizations for the prestigious "Future Food Fellow" program by the DO School in Germany. This incredible opportunity has provided us with mentoring support from internationally renowned individuals and invaluable interactions with global change-makers.

Finalist at National Startup Award

VillaMart proudly stood as a finalist in the esteemed National Startup Awards 2021, granting us the privilege of engaging with the honorable Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi. Additionally, we received invaluable handholding and mentoring support from DIPP for a duration of one year.

TATA Social Enterprise Challenge

VillaMart achieved recognition as one of the top 10 finalists in the Tata Social Enterprise Challenge 2018, organized by the esteemed Tata Group in collaboration with IIM Calcutta. This honor underscores our commitment to social entrepreneurship and innovation.
